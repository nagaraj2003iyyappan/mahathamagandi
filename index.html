Tribute to Mahatma Gandhi

Introduction :

Mahatma Gandhi, the Father of the Nation,

was a prominent leader of the Indian independence movement

against British rule. He was known for his philosophy of non-violence

and his tireless efforts to achieve social and

political change through peaceful means.

Key Achievements :

  • Leading the Salt March in 1930
  • Promoting civil disobedience and non-cooperation
  • Championing the cause of self-reliance and cottage industries
  • Advocating for equality and harmony among different communities

Legacy :

Mahatma Gandhi's legacy lives on through his teachings and principles.

His philosophy of non-violence and civil disobedience has inspired countless movements for social

justice and human rights around the world.
